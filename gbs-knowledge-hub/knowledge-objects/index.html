Asset Governance & Versioning

Every knowledge asset follows a governance lifecycle:

  • Draft: Content being developed or updated
  • Review: Submitted for subject matter expert review
  • Published: Approved and available to users
  • Retired: Superseded by newer content

Metadata & Discovery

Every asset includes rich metadata for easy discovery:

  • Tower: Which track (R2R, O2C, FP&A, etc.)
  • Role Band: 0-5, 5-10, or 10-15 years
  • Type: SOP, Template, Exception, KPI, Scenario, etc.
  • Keywords: Searchable tags and topics
  • Last Updated: Version date and update history
  • Owner: Subject matter expert contact
  • Status: Governance status (Draft, Review, Published, Retired)
