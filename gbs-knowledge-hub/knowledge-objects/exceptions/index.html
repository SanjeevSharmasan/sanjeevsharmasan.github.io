What is the Exception Library?

The Exception Library is a comprehensive catalog of the most common business exceptions encountered in GBS operations. Each exception includes root cause analysis, quick fixes, prevention measures, and escalation criteria.

Structure of Each Exception Entry

  • Exception Definition: Clear description of the issue
  • Frequency: How often it occurs
  • Impact: Business impact if not resolved
  • Root Causes: Common reasons why it happens
  • Quick Fix: Immediate resolution steps
  • Prevention: How to avoid it in the future
  • Escalation Path: Who to contact for help

Exception Categories by Track

üìä Record-to-Report (R2R) Exceptions

Reconciliation Breaks

When GL balance doesn't match subledger balance. Quick fix: Review recent entries, check for duplicates or reversals.

Journal Entry Errors

Incorrect GL posting or unbalanced entries. Prevention: Use JE templates and require approval before posting.

Late Close Activities

Month-end tasks not completed by deadline. Escalation: Notify manager, identify bottlenecks.

üí∞ Order-to-Cash (O2C) Exceptions

Unmatched Invoices

Customer doesn't recognize invoice amount. Quick fix: Check for delivery date discrepancies, verify quantities.

Deduction Disputes

Customer disputes invoice amount and takes deduction. Prevention: Clear invoicing terms, proactive communication.

Collections Aging

Invoice overdue >90 days. Escalation: Collections team to contact customer directly.

üìà Finance Planning & Analysis (FP&A) Exceptions

Forecast Misses >10%

Actual results deviate significantly from forecast. Root cause: Identify changing business conditions.

Missing Budget Data

Department hasn't submitted budget inputs by deadline. Prevention: Send reminders, enforce deadlines.
