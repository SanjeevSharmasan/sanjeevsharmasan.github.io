Scenario Overview

Situation: A customer disputes an invoice claiming they received damaged goods and should not owe the full amount. The invoice was issued 30 days ago, and the customer is threatening to withhold payment.

Your Tasks:

  • Investigate the customer's claim
  • Review the original order and shipment records
  • Assess the validity of the dispute
  • Determine resolution options
  • Document the resolution
  • Update AR records accordingly

Learning Objectives

  • Understand dispute investigation process
  • Learn how to research order and shipment details
  • Master root cause analysis for disputes
  • Understand credit memo vs. deduction handling
  • Learn documentation requirements
  • Understand AR impact of dispute resolution

Key Concepts Covered

Dispute Investigation: Gathering evidence and facts

Root Cause Analysis: Why did the dispute occur?

Resolution Options: Credit memo, replacement, or other

Customer Relations: Maintaining customer while protecting company

AR Adjustments: Recording adjustments and resolutions

Prevention: How to prevent similar disputes
