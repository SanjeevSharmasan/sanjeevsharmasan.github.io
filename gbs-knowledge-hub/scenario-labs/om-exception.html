Scenario Overview

Situation: A confirmed order has a fulfillment issue - the warehouse cannot supply one of the three requested items within the customer's delivery window. You must quickly decide on options: backorder, substitute, partial shipment, or cancel, while maintaining customer satisfaction and minimizing financial impact.

Your Tasks:

  • Assess the supply constraint
  • Understand customer requirements and timeline
  • Evaluate available options
  • Calculate financial impact of each option
  • Recommend solution and communicate with customer
  • Update order and coordinate execution

Learning Objectives

  • Master exception investigation and analysis
  • Learn problem-solving approaches
  • Understand customer communication
  • Learn impact assessment
  • Master escalation procedures
