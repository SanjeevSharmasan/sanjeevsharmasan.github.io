Scenario Overview

Situation: The annual budget cycle has begun. You are responsible for collecting financial data from four departments (Sales, Operations, HR, Finance) to compile the company-wide budget. Each department has provided data in different formats and on different schedules.

Your Tasks:

  • Collect budget submissions from all departments
  • Standardize and validate data
  • Identify and resolve discrepancies
  • Consolidate into master budget
  • Prepare summary for leadership
  • Document assumptions

Learning Objectives

  • Understand budgeting process and timeline
  • Learn data collection and validation techniques
  • Master spreadsheet consolidation
  • Understand budget assumptions documentation
  • Learn data quality checks
