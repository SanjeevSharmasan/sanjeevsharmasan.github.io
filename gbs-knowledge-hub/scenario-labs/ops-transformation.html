Scenario Overview

Situation: You are the VP of Finance Operations overseeing GBS across 5 towers (R2R, O2C, FP&A, P2P, Q2O). The CFO has tasked you with developing a 3-year GBS transformation strategy to reduce operating costs by 20% while improving quality and customer satisfaction. This requires changes across organization, technology, processes, and talent.

Your Tasks:

  • Develop implementation roadmap
  • Analyze financial impact and ROI
  • Plan organizational changes
  • Create executive transformation plan

Learning Objectives
