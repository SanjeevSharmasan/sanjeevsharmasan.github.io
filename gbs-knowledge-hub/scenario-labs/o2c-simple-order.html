Scenario Overview

Situation: You receive a new customer order for $50,000. The customer is a new account with standard terms. Walk through the order entry, fulfillment, and invoicing process from start to finish.

Your Tasks:

  • Verify customer credit information
  • Enter order in the system
  • Review fulfillment readiness
  • Process invoice to customer
  • Record in accounts receivable
  • Set up collections follow-up

Learning Objectives

  • Understand basic order-to-cash workflow
  • Learn customer credit verification process
  • Master order entry system navigation
  • Understand fulfillment and shipping process
  • Learn invoice creation and AR recording
  • Understand collections process basics

Key Concepts Covered

Credit Management: Basic customer credit check

Order System: Order entry and confirmation

Fulfillment: Standard order fulfillment process

Invoicing: Invoice creation and delivery

AR Recording: Journal entry posting

Collections: Payment follow-up and receipt
