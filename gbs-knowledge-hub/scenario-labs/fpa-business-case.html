Scenario Overview

Situation: The company is considering investing $5M in new accounting automation software that would reduce manual O2C and P2P processes by 40%. You need to develop a comprehensive business case to justify this investment, including ROI analysis, risk assessment, and implementation timeline.

Your Tasks:

  • Estimate cost savings from automation
  • Quantify other benefits (quality, speed, risk reduction)
  • Calculate ROI and payback period
  • Perform sensitivity analysis
  • Assess implementation risks
  • Prepare executive business case

Learning Objectives

  • Master business case development methodology
  • Learn cost-benefit analysis
  • Understand ROI and NPV calculations
  • Learn sensitivity analysis
  • Master risk assessment and mitigation
  • Learn executive communication of complex analyses
