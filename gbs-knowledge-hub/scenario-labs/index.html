About Scenario Labs

Scenario Labs are interactive, real-world case studies and simulations designed to build practical problem-solving skills. Each scenario presents a realistic business situation where you'll make decisions, see consequences, and learn from the outcomes. Scenarios are available at all three role levels and aligned with each track.
