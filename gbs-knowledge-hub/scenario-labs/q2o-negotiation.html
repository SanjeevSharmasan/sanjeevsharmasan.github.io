Scenario Overview

Situation: A strategic account (representing $10M in annual revenue) is negotiating a 3-year master agreement with aggressive terms, unique pricing structures, volume commitments, and SLA requirements. You need to assess the deal, negotiate terms protecting company interests, and prepare the contract for legal and executive approval.

Your Tasks:

  • Analyze customer's financial strength and credibility
  • Evaluate proposed contract terms
  • Model financial impact of deal
  • Identify negotiation priorities and red lines
  • Develop negotiation strategy
  • Prepare executive summary and recommendation

Learning Objectives

  • Master contract analysis and review
  • Learn negotiation strategy and tactics
  • Understand complex pricing models
  • Master financial impact modeling
  • Learn executive stakeholder management
  • Understand legal and compliance considerations
