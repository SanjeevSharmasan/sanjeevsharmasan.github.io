Scenario Overview

Situation: You are managing a strategic account with $2.5M in outstanding AR. The account has been slow paying for the past 6 months with multiple invoices now 60+ days overdue. The account manager is concerned about losing the business if you push too hard, but the company needs this cash.

Your Tasks:

  • Develop a collection strategy balancing cash needs and relationship
  • Analyze customer's financial position and payment capability
  • Design escalation approach
  • Prepare negotiation options
  • Create action plan with timeline
  • Document risk assessment

Learning Objectives

  • Understand strategic account collection management
  • Learn financial analysis for AR risk assessment
  • Master negotiation and escalation strategies
  • Understand write-off vs. recovery decisions
  • Learn relationship management in collections
  • Understand bad debt accounting

Key Concepts Covered

Financial Analysis: Assessing customer's ability to pay

Collection Strategy: Multi-step escalation approach

Negotiation Tactics: Payment plans, discounts, settlements

Relationship Balance: Keeping customer while protecting cash

Risk Management: Bad debt reserves and write-offs

Executive Communication: Reporting to leadership
