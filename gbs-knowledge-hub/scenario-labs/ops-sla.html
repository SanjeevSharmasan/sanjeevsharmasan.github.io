Scenario Overview

Situation: You are a new Finance Operations lead responsible for monitoring SLAs for the R2R process. Your team is targeting a 95% month-end close on-time rate. This month, the close will not meet that target due to a data quality issue. You need to communicate this, analyze the root cause, and implement preventive measures.

Your Tasks:

  • Quantify the SLA miss and impact
  • Communicate to leadership
  • Investigate root cause of data quality issue
  • Develop corrective action plan
  • Document preventive measures
  • Plan for next month recovery

Learning Objectives

  • Understand SLA metrics and measurement
  • Learn SLA reporting
  • Master root cause analysis
  • Learn corrective action planning
  • Understand escalation procedures
