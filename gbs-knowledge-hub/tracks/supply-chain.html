About This Track

Supply Chain encompasses the procurement, sourcing, and logistics network that keeps operations running efficiently. This track will focus on end-to-end supply chain management including:

  • Procurement and sourcing strategies
  • Supplier management and governance
  • Purchase order creation and management
  • Inventory optimization and planning
  • Demand forecasting and planning
  • Logistics and network optimization
  • Cost reduction and contract negotiations
