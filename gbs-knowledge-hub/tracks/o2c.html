About This Track

Order-to-Cash (O2C) encompasses the complete revenue cycle from quote generation through final cash collection. This track is critical for companies with direct customer interactions and includes:

  • Quote creation and management
  • Order processing and validation
  • Fulfillment tracking and coordination
  • Invoicing and accounts receivable management
  • Collections and cash application
  • Revenue recognition and accounting
  • Customer credit management and risk
