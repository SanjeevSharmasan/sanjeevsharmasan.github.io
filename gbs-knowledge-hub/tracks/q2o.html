About This Track

Quote-to-Order (Q2O) encompasses the front-end sales process from initial quote through order creation. This track focuses on complex product configuration, dynamic pricing, and order readiness. It includes:

  • Customer quote requests and management
  • Product configuration and bill of materials
  • Pricing rules and discount management
  • Contract negotiation and terms
  • Order creation and contract binding
  • Quote-to-order conversion tracking
  • Revenue recognition setup
