About This Track

Order Management encompasses the orchestration and fulfillment of customer orders from order entry through final delivery. This track bridges sales with operations and logistics. It includes:

  • Order entry and validation
  • Inventory allocation and reservation
  • Fulfillment planning and orchestration
  • Warehouse and logistics coordination
  • Order status tracking and visibility
  • Delivery and returns management
  • Order exceptions and escalation
