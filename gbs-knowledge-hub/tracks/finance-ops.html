About This Track

Finance Operations is the backbone of GBS and Shared Services centers. This cross-functional track spans governance, control design, process optimization, and capability building. It includes:

  • GBS operating model and governance design
  • Shared Services center setup and operations
  • Process optimization and continuous improvement
  • Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and AI implementation
  • Quality assurance and control frameworks
  • Team management and talent development
  • Technology roadmaps and system integration
