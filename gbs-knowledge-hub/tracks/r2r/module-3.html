Month-End Close Overview

The month-end close is a coordinated effort involving multiple teams and systems. Success requires careful planning and execution.

Key Activities

  • Accrual processing and adjustments
  • Account reconciliations
  • Intercompany settlements
  • Journal entry posting and approval
  • Trial balance verification
  • Financial statement preparation
  • Supporting documentation and sign-off

üí° Close Timeline Management

Establish and monitor close schedules:

  • Day 1-2: Accruals and preliminary work
  • Day 3-4: Reconciliations
  • Day 5: Journal entries and adjustments
  • Day 6-7: Financial statements and final review
  • Day 8: Final approval and reporting

Common Close Issues

Be prepared to handle:

  • Late transactions or accruals
  • System-generated discrepancies
  • Unresolved reconciling items
  • Missing supporting documentation
  • Timing differences
