Types of Reconciliations

Account reconciliations are the foundation of accurate financial reporting. You need to master several types:

Bank Reconciliations

Match GL bank balance to actual bank statement. Key steps:

  • Identify timing differences (outstanding checks, deposits in transit)
  • Resolve unusual items
  • Post necessary adjustments
  • Verify balance matches

Intercompany Reconciliations

Match balances between entities. Most complex due to timing and exchange rates.

AR/AP Subledger Reconciliations

Match GL account to supporting subledger. Requires detailed analysis of unmatched items.

üí° Best Practice: Root Cause Analysis

When a reconciliation doesn't balance, systematically identify the root cause:

  1. Check for data entry errors
  2. Verify posting to correct GL accounts
  3. Look for timing differences
  4. Investigate system issues or duplicates
  5. Document all findings
