About This Track

Record-to-Report (R2R) is the cornerstone of financial operations. It encompasses the complete cycle from transaction recording through to consolidated financial reporting. This track covers:

  • Daily GL posting and transaction recording
  • Account reconciliations (bank, AP, AR, intercompany)
  • Month-end and period-end close procedures
  • Journal entries and adjustments
  • Consolidation and elimination processes
  • Financial statement preparation
  • Audit-ready documentation and controls
