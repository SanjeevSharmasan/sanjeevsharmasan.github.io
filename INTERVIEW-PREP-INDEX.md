## ðŸš¨ If You Get Stuck During Interview

### **Immediate Actions**
1. Say "That's a great question, let me think for a moment..."
2. Glance at AI assistant tool (if visible)
3. Search for related keyword
4. Take a breath and answer naturally
5. Don't read verbatim (rephrase in your own words)

### **If You Really Don't Know**
1. Be honest: "That's not my strongest area, but I would..."
2. Show your thinking process: "I'd approach it by..."
3. Ask for clarification: "Do you mean in this context...?"
4. Redirect if appropriate: "In my experience, I've focused on..."
5. Offer to research: "That's interesting, I'd love to learn more..."

### **Red Flags to Avoid**
- âŒ Don't say "I'm not sure" without context
- âŒ Don't make up technical details you're unsure about
- âŒ Don't give one-word answers
- âŒ Don't forget to relate to business impact
- âŒ Don't let nervousness show in your voice
