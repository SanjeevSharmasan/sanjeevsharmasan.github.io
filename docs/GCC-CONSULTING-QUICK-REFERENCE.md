## TOP 10 DISCOVERY QUESTIONS

1. What's bringing you to explore GCC right now?
2. Looking at your background, how do you see that preparing you for GCC?
3. When are you realistically thinking about making this move?
4. On a scale of 1-10, how much do you already know about GCC?
5. Tell me about your work style - do you prefer structure/processes or flexibility/creativity?
6. When someone gives you feedback, how do you typically react?
7. What does success look like to you in GCC? What level do you want to reach?
8. What's your biggest concern or worry about this transition?
9. What's your salary expectation in GCC?
10. If my program is $2,000 for 12 weeks + 2-3 hours homework weekly, would you be in?
