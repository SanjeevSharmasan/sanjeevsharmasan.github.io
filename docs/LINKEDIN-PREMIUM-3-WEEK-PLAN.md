## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Best sending times:** 9-11 AM IST (workday start for recipients)

2. **Personalization matters:** Reference something specific about their company or role. Generic InMails get 5% response rate. Personalized get 25-40%.

3. **Respond fast:** If someone responds to InMail, reply within 2 hours. Speed signals interest.

4. **Use Premium analytics:** See who viewed your profile, which companies are interested. Reach out to those companies next.

5. **Ask for referrals:** At end of calls, ask: "Do you know anyone else in your network tackling similar challenges?" = warm introductions

6. **Don't sell, explore:** InMails shouldn't be sales pitches. They should be "I'm exploring, you might be interesting, let's talk."

7. **Keep it short:** InMails that are 2-3 paragraphs get 5x better response than long emails.

8. **Follow up everyone:** If someone views your profile, visit theirs within a day. Many convert to InMail responses.

9. **Engage authentically:** Comment on posts, share insights. InMails from people who engage get better response.

10. **Track everything:** Note which companies respond most, which roles, which time slots. This becomes your strategy for Week 4+.
