## ⚠️ COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

1. **Being Too Cheap**
   - Don't undervalue your expertise ($200/session is fair, not high)
   - First clients might get $125, but quickly move to $200+
   - Your knowledge hub alone justifies premium pricing

2. **Taking Wrong Clients**
   - Use green/yellow/red framework from templates
   - Say NO to: unmotivated people, those with unrealistic timelines, people not willing to invest
   - Your reputation > one client fee

3. **Overcomplicating Sessions**
   - Use the templates (they work)
   - Don't try to custom-build every session
   - Templates + personalization = perfect balance

4. **Forgetting Job Search While Consulting**
   - Keep it to 8 hours/week max
   - Block specific times (admin mornings, client evenings, weekends flexible)
   - Job search is still priority (70% energy)

5. **Not Asking for Referrals/Testimonials**
   - After every successful client, ask for both
   - This is how you grow with minimal marketing
   - Testimonials build credibility fast
